What is Data Loss And Prevention?

The term "data loss prevention" (DLP) describes methods and tools intended to stop sensitive data from being accessed, used, or sent by unauthorized parties. DLP systems keep an eye on and regulate data flow both inside and outside of a company's network, protecting sensitive data including client information and intellectual property.

By implementing regulations that limit access to and use of vital data, DLP assists companies in defending against insider threats, data breaches, and unintentional data leaks. Organizations may guarantee that sensitive data is protected from internal and external threats while upholding compliance and privacy standards by utilizing encryption, content monitoring, and access controls.
