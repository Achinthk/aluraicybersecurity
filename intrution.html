What is Intrution And Prevention System?

Physical devices including servers, workstations, and networking equipment are shielded from malicious assaults and illegal access by an Intrusion Detection and Prevention System (IDPS) for hardware solutions. Hardware devices are protected against exploitation by continuously monitoring hardware flow and activity, which identifies any unusual behavior or potential vulnerabilities.

In addition to detecting possible intrusions in real time, IDPS for hardware solutions blocks attacks before they have a chance to affect your infrastructure. By defending against internal and external threats, this proactive defensive system improves security, protects private information, and guarantees the integrity of physical objects.
